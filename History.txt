== 0.0.6 (In Git)

=== New features
* Cucumber gem (and optional version) must be declared in POM. -Dcucumber.installGems no longer installs it by default.
* Added diffLists and diffHashes methods to cuke4duke.Table. Maps to Ast::Cucumber::Table.diff!
* Compatibility with cucumber 0.3.91
* Ability to specify cucumber bin with -Dcucumber.bin= or <configuration><bin> in the Maven plugin (POM).
